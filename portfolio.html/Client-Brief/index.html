"Education doesn't stop at schools"

We are dedicated to sparking interest in students to explore the world of technology. We provide a large range of workshops and classes that encourage students to learn various science and engineering concepts.

Technology literacy for all

Regardless of what our children grow up to become, having a basic understanding of how things work is vital in our technology-dependent world. We believe that learning should be fun and work to equip our next generation with life skills including critical thinking, creativity, and a maker mindset.

Innovate and Create

We encourage our students to innovate, design, and create from brainstorms at the top of their heads! From microprocessors to labs, the work they do is hard work. Their innovative ideas will be the future to what we will know as society!

Trends have shown a huge rise in opportunities for industries who work in STEM-related fields.
